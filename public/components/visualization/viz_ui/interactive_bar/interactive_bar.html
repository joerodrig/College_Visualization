Information

Description:

Ithaca Visualized displays the structure of faculty and staff members at Ithaca College based off of their public work information listed in the Ithaca College directory.

Understanding the structure:

The size of schools are based off of how many departments are located in the school

The size of a department is based off of how many people are located in the department

Understanding the colors:

Faculty and staff members who are only assigned to a single department or school are represented as a light blue dot

Faculty and staff members assigned to multiple departments are represented as a yellow dot. By clicking one of these members, you can expand the other departments or schools that they are a part of.
